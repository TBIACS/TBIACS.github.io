Kenya Starbucks Coffee

Graced with high plateaus around Mt. Kenya—the second-highest peak in Africa—and rich volcanic soil, Kenya is known for its cooperatives of smallholder farms. This coffee was processed using a fully washed technique that has a rich tradition in the region. In the cup, it has a sparkling acidity and is finished with a delicate sweetness.
